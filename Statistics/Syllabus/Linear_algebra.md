# Linear Algebra Full Syllabus (Basic to Advanced)

## 1. Basics of Linear Algebra

* Scalars, Vectors, Matrices, and Tensors
* Notation and conventions
* Types of matrices:

  * Row, Column, Square, Diagonal
  * Identity, Zero, Symmetric, Skew-Symmetric
  * Upper/Lower Triangular, Orthogonal, Hermitian
* Matrix operations:

  * Addition, Subtraction, Scalar multiplication
  * Matrix multiplication
  * Transpose, Conjugate transpose

## 2. Systems of Linear Equations

* Representation using matrices (AX = B)
* Consistency and inconsistency of systems
* Homogeneous and non-homogeneous systems
* Gaussian Elimination Method
* Gauss–Jordan Elimination
* Row Echelon Form (REF) and Reduced Row Echelon Form (RREF)

## 3. Determinants and Inverse

* Determinant of a matrix: definition and properties
* Cofactor and minor
* Cramer's Rule
* Adjoint and Inverse of a matrix
* Properties of determinant and inverse

## 4. Vector Spaces

* Definition of vector space
* Subspaces
* Linear combination
* Linear span
* Linear independence and dependence
* Basis and Dimension of a vector space

## 5. Rank and Nullity

* Rank of a matrix
* Row rank and column rank
* Null space and nullity
* Rank-Nullity Theorem

## 6. Linear Transformation

* Definition and properties
* Kernel and Image of transformation
* Matrix representation of linear transformation
* Composition of transformations
* Change of basis
* Similarity of matrices

## 7. Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors

* Definition and computation
* Characteristic polynomial
* Diagonalization of a matrix
* Algebraic and geometric multiplicity
* Eigenspaces
* Cayley-Hamilton Theorem
* Application: Power method for dominant eigenvalue

## 8. Orthogonality and Inner Product Spaces

* Dot product and inner product
* Orthogonal and orthonormal vectors
* Orthogonal projection
* Gram-Schmidt Orthogonalization
* QR decomposition
* Least squares solution to inconsistent systems

## 9. Advanced Topics

* Spectral Theorem for symmetric matrices
* Singular Value Decomposition (SVD)
* Positive definite, semi-definite matrices
* Norms and condition numbers
* Pseudoinverse (Moore-Penrose inverse)
* Matrix decompositions: LU, QR, Cholesky, SVD

## 10. Applications in Machine Learning and Data Science

* Principal Component Analysis (PCA)
* Dimensionality Reduction
* Covariance matrices
* Linear regression (normal equations)
* Optimization in vector spaces
