# Calculus Full Syllabus (Basic to Advanced)

## 1. Functions and Limits

* Definition of a function
* Domain and range
* Types of functions (polynomial, rational, exponential, logarithmic, trigonometric)
* Graphs of functions
* Limit of a function
* One-sided limits
* Properties of limits
* Limit laws
* Limits at infinity
* Continuity and types of discontinuities

## 2. Differentiation

* Definition of derivative
* Geometric interpretation (slope of tangent)
* Rules of differentiation

  * Sum, product, quotient, and chain rules
* Derivatives of elementary functions
* Higher order derivatives
* Implicit differentiation
* Logarithmic differentiation
* Parametric differentiation

## 3. Applications of Derivatives

* Increasing and decreasing functions
* Maxima and minima (local and absolute)
* Critical points and inflection points
* Mean Value Theorem (MVT)
* Rolle's Theorem
* Taylor and Maclaurin series
* Newton’s method for roots
* Curve sketching using derivatives
* Optimization problems

## 4. Integration

* Indefinite integrals
* Basic integration formulas
* Methods of integration:

  * Substitution
  * Integration by parts
  * Partial fractions
  * Trigonometric substitution
* Definite integrals
* Properties of definite integrals
* Fundamental Theorem of Calculus

## 5. Applications of Integration

* Area under curves
* Area between two curves
* Volume of solids of revolution (Disk, Washer, Shell methods)
* Arc length of curves
* Surface area of solids of revolution
* Average value of a function
* Center of mass and moment of inertia (intro level)

## 6. Differential Equations (Intro Level)

* Basic definitions and terminology
* Separable differential equations
* First-order linear differential equations
* Applications in physics and population models

## 7. Multivariable Calculus

* Functions of several variables
* Partial derivatives
* Total derivative
* Gradient, directional derivatives
* Chain rule for multivariable functions
* Tangent planes and normal vectors
* Maxima, minima, and saddle points
* Lagrange multipliers

## 8. Multiple Integration

* Double and triple integrals
* Iterated integrals
* Change of variables (Jacobian)
* Applications: volume, mass, center of gravity

## 9. Vector Calculus

* Vector fields
* Line integrals
* Conservative vector fields
* Green’s Theorem
* Divergence and Curl
* Surface integrals
* Stokes' Theorem
* Divergence Theorem

## 10. Applications in Engineering, Physics, and Machine Learning

* Gradient descent in optimization
* Calculus in motion, heat, and waves
* Backpropagation in neural networks (using chain rule)
* Continuous probability distributions and integration
