# Statistics Full Syllabus (Basic to Advanced)

## 1. Introduction to Statistics

* Definition and scope of statistics
* Types of data: qualitative vs quantitative
* Scales of measurement: nominal, ordinal, interval, ratio
* Population vs sample
* Types of sampling methods

## 2. Descriptive Statistics

* Measures of central tendency: mean, median, mode
* Measures of dispersion: range, variance, standard deviation, IQR
* Moments and measures of shape:

  * Skewness
  * Kurtosis
* Frequency distributions and histograms
* Box plots and visual summaries

## 3. Probability Theory

* Basic concepts: experiment, sample space, event
* Types of events: independent, dependent, mutually exclusive
* Axioms of probability
* Conditional probability and Bayes' theorem
* Permutations and combinations

## 4. Random Variables and Probability Distributions

* Discrete and continuous random variables
* Probability mass function (PMF) and probability density function (PDF)
* Cumulative distribution function (CDF)
* Expectation (mean), variance, standard deviation
* Common distributions:

  * Bernoulli, Binomial, Poisson
  * Uniform, Normal, Exponential

## 5. Joint, Marginal, and Conditional Distributions

* Joint probability distribution (discrete and continuous)
* Marginal probability
* Conditional distributions
* Covariance and correlation
* Independence of random variables

## 6. Sampling and Sampling Distributions

* Law of large numbers
* Central Limit Theorem (CLT)
* Sampling distributions of sample mean and sample proportion
* Standard error

## 7. Estimation

* Point estimation and properties of estimators
* Confidence intervals for:

  * Population mean
  * Population proportion
  * Difference of means/proportions
* Sample size determination

## 8. Hypothesis Testing

* Null and alternative hypothesis
* Type I and Type II errors
* p-values and significance level
* One-tailed and two-tailed tests
* Tests for means, proportions, variances (Z-test, t-test, chi-square test, F-test)

## 9. Analysis of Variance (ANOVA)

* One-way ANOVA
* Two-way ANOVA
* Assumptions and interpretation

## 10. Regression and Correlation

* Simple linear regression
* Multiple linear regression
* Correlation coefficient
* Coefficient of determination (R-squared)
* Assumptions of regression models

## 11. Non-parametric Methods

* Sign test, Wilcoxon test
* Mann-Whitney U test
* Kruskal-Wallis test
* Chi-square test for independence

## 12. Time Series and Forecasting

* Components of time series
* Moving averages
* Exponential smoothing
* Trend and seasonality analysis
* ARIMA models (intro level)

## 13. Statistical Quality Control

* Control charts for variables and attributes
* Process capability analysis
* Acceptance sampling

## 14. Applications in Data Science and Machine Learning

* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Feature selection based on correlation and variance
* Statistical significance in ML models
* Confidence intervals for model performance
* Bootstrap methods
* A/B testing and experimentation
